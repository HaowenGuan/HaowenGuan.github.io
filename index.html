Welcome to My Homepage

This place is where all my crazy ideas and knowledge live

My name is Haowen Guan, mandarin written as 关皓文. I am from Shenzhen, China.

I graduated from University of Washington in 2021 and held a bachelor degree double major in Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics: Data Science. At the same year, I started my graduate school at New York University major in Data Science. I am expecting to graduate by Jun, 2023.

I am a physics nerd. I love physics in a way that I took it as the shortest path to approach the truth of the world. During undergraduate study, I realized we are living in an era of information explosion, such that the data and computation resources is increasing exponentially every year. It reminded me the importance of a skill to master data and digital device, which led me to Data Science, as it is a field aiming to fuse big data and artificial intelligence. I love Data Science, and I believe it is the greatest "hammer" of this era, which can push forward every academic, including physics.
